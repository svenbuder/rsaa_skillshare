# RSAA Skillshare Series

This repository hosts materials for the **RSAA Skillshare**, a monthly, hands-on series for students and postdocs at RSAA.

The Skillshare provides an informal space to share practical research skills, professional development tools, and effective workflows that are often learned implicitly rather than taught explicitly. Materials will be added here throughout the year.

## Format

- **When:** Second Tuesday of each month, 3:00–4:30ish pm  
- **Where:** CSO Common Room  
- **Structure:** Short introduction followed by panels or hands-on activities  

Dates are also listed in the RSAA seminars calendar.

## Who is this for?

- Honours and Masters students  
- PhD students (all stages)  
- Postdoctoral researchers  

Supervisors and lecturers are encouraged to support student participation.  
Anyone interested in contributing a session or panel is very welcome.
